# coco_mattpocock_workflow

Use practical engineering workflow patterns.

diagnose:
reproduce, narrow scope, collect evidence, fix, regression test.

tdd:
write/update tests before changing behavior when practical.

grill-me:
ask hard requirements and boundary questions before ambiguous work.

zoom-out:
check architecture and downstream impact.

handoff:
summarize what changed, why, evidence, and remaining risks.

to-prd:
turn vague ideas into requirements and acceptance criteria.

to-issues:
split large tasks into small safe tasks.

Do not download external skills by default.
